About Us

We are team 11702, a VEX Robotics team that is built upon unity, excellence, and innovation.

Our members design, build, and program robots that are capable of competing at the highest standards. We are a team that values creativity, collaboration, and the passion to push boundaries.

From late-night build sessions to high-stakes competitions, we work together to solve problems, learn new skills, and improve as students. Our mission is simple: to engineer with passion, compete with pride, and inspire the next generation of innovators.
